MANPOWER SPECIALIZED SERVICES

We, at Solutions Energy Services’ understand the intricacies of human resource management.

We are dedicated to bridge the gap between the requirement of our clients and the dedicated individuals. Our philosophy is to provide our clients with the best service.

We believe in “Right people for the Right job”.

We offer Manpower Services for the following categories:

  • Engineers Engineers (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Instrumentation), Project Engineers, QA/QC Inspectors, Site Engineers, Safety Engineers, Planning Engineers, Material Engineers, Cost Engineers and Turnover Engineers.
