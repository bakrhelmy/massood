HARD BANDING

Solutions Energy Services’ provides Hard Banding services for all kinds of DP, HWDP and BHA for all sizes of drilling pipes by using specially designed CNC controled & advanced Hard Banding Machines, which enable us to maintain the exact parameters of 18 degree tilting and we rework the 18 degree taper on the drill pipes as well.

  • Our System offers a comprehensive solution for hard banding services, which are fully equipped with the latest technology of hydraulic drilling pipes handling systems, which can handle all kinds of heavy weight drilling pipes and drill collars effortlessly.
  • Solutions Energy Services’ hard bands are currently applied using GMAW (gas metal arc weld) welding process, a mobile hard banding unit to assist the clients in reducing the overall transport costs and tubular handling costs.
  • We are licensed by the World’s leading Hard Banding solution providing companies such as;
  • 150XT
  • 350XT
  • Duraband
  • Tuffband
  • Ultraband nm (NON MAG)
